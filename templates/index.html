The Problem

Documents pile up.
Answers don't.

Search finds words, not answers. It won't connect what's spread across your formats, tell you what a document really means, or show what changed between versions.

🗂️
Mixed formats, no common ground

Numbers in a sheet, terms in a PDF, notes in a doc. Nothing connects them into a single answer.

🧾
The gist takes too long

Getting the who, what and when from a long document is slow, and skimming makes you miss what matters.

🕓
Versions drift silently

Spotting what changed between two files is manual and error-prone. DocMind diffs them page by page.

Two retrieval engines
